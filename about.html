Our Mandate

Redefining safety parameters with smart technology

Stackly was established in 2014 with a clear purpose: to bridge the gap between static camera recordings and active, real-time threat prevention.

We believe security shouldn't be reactive. Simply viewing footage of an incident after it occurred is a failure of system architecture. By deploying artificial intelligence analytics at the edge, we identify potential intrusions, dispatch responders, and alert owners in real time.

Quick Answers

Support Parameters

Does Stackly support third-party hardware integration?
Yes. While we recommend our UL-listed cameras and control boards, our software platform utilizes standard ONVIF protocol specifications, enabling integration with existing modern IP security cameras.
Is a contract required for remote command monitoring?
Yes, our 24/7 proactive dispatch monitoring packages operate on a standard 12-month agreement. This contract locks in your monthly rate and covers continuous firmware updates, cloud backups, and regular physical diagnostic checks.
