How long does a standard commercial installation take?
Most commercial installations take between 2 to 5 business days. This includes running secure, low-voltage wires, installing wall mounts, configuring network servers, and training your security team on the control dashboard interface.
Does the CCTV feed work during a power outage?
Yes. All Stackly premium installations include a dedicated Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS) backup system. This guarantees that your sensors, cameras, and local transmission systems remain fully operational for up to 12 hours during an outage.
How is my monitoring video stream secured?
All video transmissions utilize high-grade AES-256 military encryption. Access key tokens are strictly held at your dashboard terminal, ensuring that neither hackers nor outside parties can intercept your live security video feed.
